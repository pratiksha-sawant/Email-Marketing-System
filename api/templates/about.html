MovieLens is run by GroupLens, a research lab at the University of Minnesota. It is non-commercial, site which helps you find movies you will like. They rate movies to build a custom taste profile,then recommends other movies for you to watch. Our objective is to offer a web application which will let marketing team carry out email-marketing campaigns and perform analytics through interactive dashboards. We present you our email marketing and analytics system which recommends movies to our users by using Azure Machine Learning Service. The recommendations are shared on emails from a third party domain Mailgun which tracks all the emails and gives statistics behind every delivered and undelivered email. We also provide analytics behind the change in user activities after sending recommendations
